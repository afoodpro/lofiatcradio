## Design Context

### Users
People working or studying with the player open in the background. Occasionally shift to intentional listening. The interface is peripheral — it should never demand attention, but reward it when attention arrives.

### Brand Personality
Calm. Melancholic. Atmospheric. Like an empty airport terminal at 2am — functional, a little lonely, strangely beautiful.

### Aesthetic Direction
Warm minimalism. Beige and off-white surfaces, dark charcoal accents. No bright colors, no energy. The interface should feel like it exists slightly outside of time.
Theme: light (daytime work context, warm paper tones).
Anti-references: anything energetic, gamified, or neon. No gradients, no glow effects.

### Design Principles
1. **Stillness over spectacle** — animations should be slow and nearly invisible. Motion that draws attention has failed.
2. **Melancholy is not sadness** — the interface can feel a little quiet and wistful, not broken or cold.
3. **Peripheral-first** — works beautifully when not looked at. Rewards a glance.
4. **No friction** — nothing should feel like it needs to be operated. It just plays.
